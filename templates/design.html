Stock research should feel understandable before it feels powerful.

Stock research can get overwhelming fast. A lot of platforms are built like you already know exactly what every chart, indicator, screener, and data table means. That works for experienced traders, but it makes things harder for beginners who are just trying to understand what they are looking at.

VSN keeps the important pieces in one place You shouldn't need five different tabs open just to follow one stock, compare the basics, or understand why something is moving.

We also don't believe basic stock tools should be locked behind a paywall. There are no free trials that turn into surprise subscriptions, and there are no features that suddenly become paid once you start using them. If something is on VSN, the goal is for people to actually be able to use it.

This is a small project built by a small developer team, not a giant company trying to sell another finance subscription. That means we can move quickly, fix issues often, and listen when something feels confusing, broken, or unnecessary.

VSN is not trying to replace every advanced trading platform. There is a time and a place for those high-end tools, but we believe there's also a place for a simpler, more focused approach. VSN is for people who want a cleaner place to track stocks, understand basic market information, and build confidence without getting buried under tools they don't need yet.
